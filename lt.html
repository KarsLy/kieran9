My School



I go to Lane Tech, a top high school in Chicago. Lane Tech College Prep High School is a large, selective enrollment magnet school in Chicago known for strong academics, diverse programs (STEM, AP, Arts, JROTC), and a strong athletic tradition, serving grades 7-12 with a focus on preparing students for college and careers. It's one of the oldest and largest high schools in Illinois, offering extensive AP courses and unique programs like aquaponics, while fostering a supportive, championship-focused environment for its thousands of students.
